How to Use Student's Cookbook
Getting Started

Welcome to Student's Cookbook. This website helps you find recipes based on ingredients you already have.

How It Works
  • 1. Add Ingredients: Type any ingredient in the search box and press Enter to add it to your list.
  • 2. Find Recipes: Click "Find Recipes" to search for dishes you can make.
  • 3. Explore Results: Browse recipes organized by how well they match your available ingredients.
